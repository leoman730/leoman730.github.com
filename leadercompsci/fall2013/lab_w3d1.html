Intro to Scratch!

Set up a Scratch account to start writing and sharing code online! Follow the steps below. You will turn in your lab by submitting the form in the last step.

  1. Go to http://scratch.mit.edu/
  2. Click on the "Join Scratch" button at the top of the screen, and follow the steps. Choose a username and password that you will remember.
  3. Once you have created an account, click on "Try out starter projects" in the "Welcome to Scratch!" box.
  4. Select any starter project and click the "See Inside" button to see the code that created this project.
  5. Now create your own remix of this project by pressing the "Remix" button.
  6. Use the rest of the lab time to explore the features of Scratch create an interesting remix of the starter project.
  7. Share your remix by hitting the share button. (You will need the URL to your shared remix for the lab submission form.)
  8. During the last 5 minutes of class, fill out this form to submit your lab: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1IhfBNr8V9xA8DfQjyTuRtY732sFz7pBdM5eUJ68YyB0/viewform
