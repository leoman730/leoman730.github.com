Lab: Guessing Game

In this lab, you will create number guessing game in Scratch. Topics in this lab: variables, conditional logic, random operator, and comparison operators.

Directions:

  1. The game has already been started for you here. Open the link to the Scratch project and log in to your account by clicking the 'Sign In' link at the top of the page.
  2. Once you are signed in, click the 'See Inside' button at the top right of the page to view the project code. Then click the 'Remix' button to start making your game from this project.
  3. Follow the steps in the comment on the main sprite of the project. (If you get stuck, check out the hint sprites.)
  4. Be sure to share your project by clicking the 'Share' button at the top right of the page so the project can be graded!
  5. Save time at the end of class to fill out his form to submit your lab.
