Lab: Make Some Noise

Directions:

  1. Practice using loops by creating your own beats. Get creative! Here's the project you will be remixing: http://scratch.mit.edu/projects/12650440/. It should look very different after you are done with it.
  2. Sign into your Scratch account
  3. After you sign in, look inside the lab project
  4. Remix the project to create your own copy to modify
  5. Use loops and sound blocks to create you own beats!
  6. Share your project
  7. Save time at the end of class to fill out the form to submit your lab: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1bqtSZkJ5SnM-PTktMcoVkiy8Vlomq2JxdAuNVnZ5eBI/viewform
