Lab: Mario 1

Start your Mario Project!

  1. Go to the Scratch site: http://scratch.mit.edu/ and sign into your account
  2. Here's the link to the assignment: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1xd-BNQ8mKTkGd3iJG_vPTXmY-iNGSOvQk7dYF6EydzU/edit?usp=sharing.
  3. Here's a basic template with some sprites that you should use for Mario and the environment: http://scratch.mit.edu/projects/12790380/.
  4. Save time at the end of class to fill out the form to submit your lab: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1iIBjLb_7HsF4G0I9OYmwiw1a7k1G5DL1HMoFcPJBtHs/viewform

    Make sure you share your project before you submit the link!

    To submit the link in the form, select the url in your web browser and copy and paste it into the form. On macs, hold down the 'command' and 'c' keys to copy and the 'command' and 'v' keys to paste.
