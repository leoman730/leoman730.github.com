Lab: Fake Space Invaders Clones

In this lab, you will use your new knowledge of cloning to make a space invaders game with multiple invaders.

Directions:

  1. Log in to Snap! from snap.berkeley.edu.
  2. Create a new account by clicking on the Cloud icon.
  3. Check your email to confirm your new account, then go back to Snap! and log in.
  4. Copy the space invader, spaceship and missile sprites from the old Scratch lab, and load them into your Snap! project.
  5. Save your lab by clicking on the File icon, then clicking "Save." Make sure to click the "Cloud" icon and ALSO click "Share". This is necessary for us to open and review your projects. Thanks!
  6. Save time at the end of class to fill out his form and submit your lab.
