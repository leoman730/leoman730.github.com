Input and Control

This week, we'll be working with input and control. By the end of the week, you'll be able to control multiple sprites via the keyboard and simulate gravity.

  1. Go to http://scratch.mit.edu/ and log in to your account.
  2. Start a new project.
  3. Add a sprite to your stage. (Choose a sprite that is facing either left or right, not straight ahead.) Add scripts to your sprite so that when you press the left arrow, the sprite faces left AND walks left.
  4. Add scripts to your sprite so that whne you press the right arrow, the sprite faces right AND walks right.
  5. Add a new sprite to your stage to represent the ground.
  6. Add scripts to your sprite so that if you drag it to a spot above the ground and press the green flag, it falls back down to the ground.
  7. Finally, add scripts to your sprite so that if you press spacebar, it jumps up 100 units.
  8. At the end of class, fill out this form to submit your lab: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1BukfRNGc-YWrFMvrR7zQgDGhXaFq-9ncS4yxF8Vij-Q/viewform.
