Input and Control, Part 2

This week, we'll be working with input and control. By the end of the week, you'll be able to control multiple sprites via the keyboard and simulate gravity.

  1. Go to http://scratch.mit.edu/ and log in to your account.
  2. Continue working on your project from yesterday -- you need to get gravity working the way it was explained yesterday.
  3. Add sprites to your page that represent platforms.
  4. Modify your code so that gravity will check if your sprite is touching the ground or any platform.
