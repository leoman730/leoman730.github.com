Loops

This week, we'll be working with loops. By the end of the week, you'll be able to build programs that use loops and sound blocks to create unique synthesized music.

  1. Fill out this survey.
  2. Go to http://scratch.mit.edu/ and log in to your account.
  3. Open and save a new copy of your project from yesterday (check the File menu).
  4. Continue working on the project, focusing on adding multiple sprites that move/dance simultaneously, and adding multiple backdrops to the stage.
  5. At the end of class, fill out the form to submit your lab.
