Variables II: Fahrenheit to Celsius

Creating mathematical formulas is easier when you use variables. Let's make a simple fahrenheit to celcius calculator to demonstrate.

  1. Go to http://scratch.mit.edu/ and log in to your account.
  2. Remix this project. Don't forget to share it!
  3. Fill in the code to calculate the temperature in celcius after the user inputs it in fahrenheit. Use variables to store the constant values in the formula and any intermediate values that you have to compute.
  4. At the end of class, fill out this form to submit your lab.
