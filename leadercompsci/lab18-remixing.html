Lab: Remixing Lists

In this lab, you will use your new knowledge of lists to build two functions: maximum of and average of.

Directions:

  1. Go to Scratch and find an interesting project that uses lists.
  2. How and why is the project using lists? What list functions is it using, and why?
  3. Now remix the lab, and add a feature to it.
  4. Once you've completed your lab, submit your lab via this form.
