An UX PROJECT BY PATRICK HUANG
Through in-depth research, I found that shoppers prioritize deals and convenience. Leveraging this insight, I designed, prototyped and tested a shopping app to help malls see an increase in salses and customer satisfaction.

Competitive Analysis

From my competitive analysis, I learned that although users want to use some sort of mobile application during their mall visit, there doesn’t seem to be one that can meet their expectations.

  • Turn by turn direction
  • Booking for movies and resturants

Target Users

By synthesized the data I collected from survey and user interviews, I created two personas that the users in the application.

Busy Emily

“Finding deals and save time is very important when I shopping at the mall.”

Background:

35, female, Fashion designer College married with 2 kids

Goals:

- Get thing done fast - Buying the right product

Motivation:

Emily is a busy person who has a full time job as a fashion designer. She usually reserve her weekend to family bonding. Her family has many needs and she always spend money smartly. Shopping at a local mall has been a monthly routine and a one stop shop for her for the past five years. 

Frustrations:

- She has a difficult time to find good deals at the mall - Shopping at the mall is time consuming and confusing
